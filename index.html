🔍 Research & Problem Definition

Community Problem Analysis

Identified recurring patterns in fitness adoption challenges through community surveys and observational studies. Key issues included lack of personalization, motivation barriers, and accessibility concerns.

Literature Review

Conducted systematic review of existing research on fitness app effectiveness, AI integration in health applications, and behavioral psychology in exercise adoption.

Opportunity Mapping

Synthesized research findings to identify opportunities for AI-powered solutions that address real user needs and community challenges.

🎯 Key Research Insights

📊 User Evaluation & Testing

🗣️ Qualitative Methods

  • User Interviews: In-depth sessions with 15 target users
  • Think-Aloud Protocol: Observational testing during prototype interaction
  • Focus Groups: Group discussions on AI features and user experience
  • Contextual Inquiry: Understanding fitness routines in natural settings

📈 Quantitative Methods

  • Task Completion Rates: Measured across core user journeys
  • Time-to-Completion: Efficiency metrics for key tasks
  • Error Rates: Tracking user mistakes and recovery paths
  • System Usability Scale: Standardized usability assessment
